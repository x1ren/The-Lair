# The Lair - Complete Character Guide

## Player Characters

### 1. Iben Anoos — The Codebreaker

**Role**: High-damage glass cannon specializing in logic and precision

**Base Stats:**
- **HP**: 400 (700 after initialization)
- **Logic**: 200-300 (randomized, high)
- **Wisdom**: 200
- **Character Class**: The Codebreaker

**Description:**
> "A quiet thinker who sees through syntax like glass."

**Playstyle**: High-risk, high-reward. Iben Anoos excels at dealing massive damage through logic-based attacks but has lower HP, requiring careful positioning and skill management.

**Skills:**
1. **Debugger's Eye** (40 Wisdom, 3-turn cooldown)
   - Reduces enemy defense by 25% for 3 turns
   - Reveals hidden abilities
   - Enables synergy with Syntax Slayer

2. **Syntax Slayer** (60 Wisdom, 2-turn cooldown)
   - Deals Logic × 1.5 damage
   - Deals Logic × 2.0 damage if enemy defense is weakened (synergy)
   - High burst damage skill

3. **Loop Mastery** (80 Wisdom, 4-turn cooldown)
   - Repeats last skill for free
   - Allows combo execution
   - Strategic skill reset

**Best For**: Players who enjoy high-damage builds and strategic skill combinations.

## Stat System Explained

### HP (Health Points)
- Determines how much damage you can take before defeat
- Restored by items (Potions) or skills
- Increases with level-ups and stage upgrades

### Logic
- Primary damage stat for attacks
- Affects skill damage calculations
- Can be buffed by skills and status effects
- Increases with level-ups and stage upgrades

### Wisdom
- Resource for using skills (like MP/Mana)
- Required to cast signature skills
- Restored by items (Ethers) or skills
- Increases with level-ups and stage upgrades

### Experience & Leveling
- Gain XP from defeating enemies and completing puzzles
- Level 2 requires 200 XP
- Subsequent levels require 200 × current level XP
- Each level grants random stat increases
